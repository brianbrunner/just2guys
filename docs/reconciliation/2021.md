## Canonical interpretation

The season operated as two conferences:

1. `Just 2 Guys - Evens` (`737467001143988224`) and `Just 2 Guys - Odds` (`737466838908329984`) supplied the regular season and conference playoffs through Week 16.
2. `Evens 2: Eventric Boogaloo` (`781405019735105536`) and `2 Odds 2 Furiodds` (`781384564496064512`) were repair leagues created after the original leagues had been configured to end too early.
3. The repair leagues supplied Week 17 lineups and scores for the two cross-conference title games: ultimate champion and ultimate last place.
4. The separate sixteen-team `Just 2 Guys` candidate (`720897275471155200`) has materially different scores and is not part of this two-conference interpretation.

The unfinished legacy `sleeper.py` explicitly selected an original conference league and a late-created repair league. The owner independently recalled switching to the repair leagues once the conference playoffs had produced their finalists. Those facts agree with the snapshot pattern: normal original-league brackets through Week 16 and complete repair-league scores in Week 17.
