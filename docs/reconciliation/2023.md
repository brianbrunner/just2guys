# 2023 Week 14 score reconciliation

Status: reviewed and incorporated into season manifest version 2  
Evidence captured: 2026-07-21 from the frozen public Sleeper REST snapshot  
Owner confirmation: the league has no legitimate tied games

## Diagnosis

Sleeper league `995467206685184000` reports two score fields for every Week 14 roster:

- `points` contains the populated platform score produced by the lineup.
- `custom_points` is exactly `0` for all fourteen rosters.

The first importer treated any non-null `custom_points` value as a replacement score. That converted the entire seven-game slate into false 0–0 ties. No other source week in the frozen Sleeper history has a non-null `custom_points` value, and no two populated platform scores are equal.
