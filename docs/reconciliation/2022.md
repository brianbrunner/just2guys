## Canonical interpretation

The season again operated as two conferences:

1. `Just 2 Guys - whiskeys` (`864551860646821888`) and `Just 2 Guys - tequilas` (`864551551908352000`) supplied regular-season play and independent conference brackets through Week 16.
2. Each conference bracket produced one champion and one loser.
3. The unpaired Week 17 scores in those same leagues supplied the cross-conference ultimate championship and ultimate last-place games.
4. The empty sixteen-team rollover (`860767078171414528`) supplied no scored games and is not canonical. Its populated template bracket does not match the owner-described conference format.

This interpretation explains all sixteen managers, unlike the empty rollover bracket, and directly matches the owner's recollection that the conference winners and losers were compared only in Week 17.
